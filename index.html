Galaxy Gymnastics

ABOUT


Opening on October 1st, 2016 as the newest gymnastics facility in Salisbury, Maryland! We are committed to providing excellent gymnastics instruction to children of all abilities.

Here at Galaxy Gymnastics we are part of a large program of athletic instruction. Not only do we offer gymnastics instruction, but we also offer competitive cheerleading through Nor’Eastern Storm, competitive dance through Nor’Eastern Storm, and tumbling through Galaxy Athletics.

Our mission at Galaxy Gymnastics is to provide top quality gymnastics instruction to children as well as allow them to have fun. We provide a safe place for our athletes to learn skills that will help them develop into the best gymnast they can be.

The strength, flexibility, and coordination that gymnastics teaches are just the physical skills that our athletes gain. Gymnastics teaches goal setting, determination, confidence, and focus which are skills that will help your child grow into a successful adult.

Our staff members are dedicated to the safety and well being of your children. We are here to teach your children gymnastics and use our knowledge to help them be challenged individually. Each of us have extensive gymnastics knowledge as well as knowledge of child development. Our goal is to provide what your child needs so that they can develop into the best gymnast they can be. Our instructors are certified and background checked though USA Gymnastics.

Preschool Gymnastics

Gymnastics for preschoolers is proven to help with kindergarten readiness as well as development of large motor skills. At Galaxy our preschool-aged classes are designed specifically for the age-group that we are teaching.

Recreational Gymnastics

We provide a variety of recreational classes throughout the week that help your child learn gymnastics to their fullest potential. There are different levels of classes to fit your child's specific needs. Classes are offered at different days and times.

Competitive Gymnastics

At Galaxy we offer all levels of competitive gymnastics that will provide the best training possible to help your child get to their highest level.

Birthday Parties and Special Events

Birthday Parties at Galaxy Gymnastics are the perfect way for your child to celebrate their birthday in a fun and active way! We also accommodate field trips and special events upon request.

Registration

The registration fee is $15 and covers your entire family for the year. In order to sign up for a class we require that you pay the registration fee. This also gives you a half-price discount to open gym.

Signing up by the session gives your child a better chance of success than just over the course of the month. It also gives you a discount per class by committing to be in class for 10 or 11 weeks.

Who We Are? Meet Our Team!

Chelsea Lahey

Chelsea was a competitive gymnast until the age of 14 and then switched over to competitive cheerleading. She cheered on the world renowed cheerleading team Maryland Twister F5 where she was part of a national championship team, received airtime on America's Got Talent and ESPN, and finished 4th place at the Cheerleading Worlds. Chelsea has worked with Pam Duke on and off, coaching cheerleading and gymnastics for the past 10 years. She can't wait to continue this new journey with her.

Pam Duke

Pam's love for gymnastics began at a young age when she started competing in the sport under the well known Bob Morris. From there, her interest grew into not only gymnastics, but cheerleading too. She started her coaching career with varsity cheerleading at James M. Bennett High School from 1993 - 1996. From 1996 - 2000, she coached Varsity Cheerleading at Parkside High School, where she developed a competitive team. She then coaced at Salisbury University from 2000 - 2004. In 2006, she began Nor' Eastern Storm All Stars.

Ally Lewis

Ally is a senior at Salisbury Univeristy with a major in Elementary Education and a minor in Creatinve Arts. She plays club soccer and was on the SU Gymnastics team for one year. She is the team manager fo the SU club Ice Hockey team and an active participant in a variety of intramural sports. She has a passion for working with kids and encouraging them to have fun.
